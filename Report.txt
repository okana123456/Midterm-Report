    <!-- 2. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY -->
    <h2>1. Executive Summary</h2>
    <p>
        The Reproductive Health Network Kenya (RHNK) stands at a pivotal moment in its organizational history as it concludes the first half of its ambitious 2022‚Äì2027 Strategic Plan. This Mid-Term Review (MTR) serves not merely as a retrospective evaluation but as a strategic compass intended to guide the organization through the complex landscape of Sexual and Reproductive Health and Rights (SRHR) in Kenya over the coming years. The review period, spanning from January 2022 to January 2026, has been characterized by significant growth, resilience in the face of external challenges, and an unwavering commitment to the vision of a healthy society with comprehensive health information for all.
    </p>
    <p>
        The findings of this evaluation unequivocally demonstrate that RHNK has solidified its position as a premier provider of SRHR advocacy and services. Through a robust network of private providers, community engagement, and strategic policy influence, the organization has achieved and, in several instances, surpassed its mid-term targets. The most notable achievement is the expansion of service delivery, with the network now facilitating high-quality care to over <span class="blue-highlight">4.4 million Kenyans annually</span>. This figure represents a substantial increase from the baseline and reflects the effectiveness of the network's decentralized service delivery model.
    </p>

    <p>
        Financial resilience has also been a hallmark of this period. Despite a contracting global funding environment, RHNK successfully diversified its donor base, increasing its annual revenue by approximately 35 percent. However, the review also identifies critical areas necessitating recalibration. The reliance on project-specific funding remains a risk to long-term sustainability, and while data systems have improved, the integration of digital health solutions across all member facilities requires accelerated effort.
    </p>

    <!-- 3. INTRODUCTION AND BACKGROUND -->
    <h2>2. Introduction and Background</h2>
    <h3>2.1 About RHNK: Organizational Overview</h3>
    <p>
        Founded in 2010, the Reproductive Health Network Kenya (RHNK) is a legally registered network that has grown into Kenya's premier sexual and reproductive health and rights organization. RHNK operates as a collaborative platform bringing together over 600 trained health professionals from private and public health facilities across 44 out of Kenya's 47 counties.
    </p>
